Warm up

  • 10x Alternating arm reaches
  • 10x Arm circles forward
  • 10x arm circles backward
  • Slow controlled toe touch
  • Downward dog alternating legs
  • 10x hamstring sweeps
  • 15 sec quad stretch left and right

Cool down

  • 15 sec Across the arm stretch right
  • 15 sec tricep, behind head left
  • 15 sec tricep, behind head right
  • 20 sec behind back clasped together arm
  • 20 sec glute stretch left
  • 20 sec glute stretch right
  • 20 sec Childs pose
  • 20 sec quad stretch left
  • 20 sec quad stretch right
